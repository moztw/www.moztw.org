什麼是MozTW Lab？
What is MozTW Lab ?

台北這邊的 Lab 從來都不是為了有什麼目標或者要作什麼事情而聚的，有新消息聊、有困難找人討論，但不需要有統一的目標。 -- BobChao，〈台北的 MozTW Lab，都在幹麼？〉

We do not need a certain goal or something to do for Lab at Taipei. There is new information to discuss, and difficulties to solve, but there is no unified target. -- BobChao, 〈What did we do in MozTW Lab at Taipei?〉
